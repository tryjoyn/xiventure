Our Conviction

Something fundamental is shifting in how work gets done. Not overnight. Not all at once. But unmistakably.

For most of modern history, scale belonged to organizations. Big ambitions required big teams. Coordination, headcount, infrastructure were the price of doing anything meaningful.

That relationship is changing.

The tools available to an individual today — to think, build, research, communicate, execute — at a level that once required entire departments — are unlike anything before. We are at the beginning of an era where the gap between individual ambition and individual capability starts to close.

That is not a threat to work. It is the largest expansion of human potential we have seen in a generation.

"The most powerful companies of the next decade won't be the biggest ones. They'll be the most capable ones."
